About us

Three people, one shared notebook of trips.

Wander Log started as a shared notebook passed between three friends after every trip. A few years and a few dozen stamped passports later, it turned into a small website: no ads to scroll past, no algorithm deciding what you see first, just the places we'd genuinely send a friend to.

  • 01

    We go before we recommend

    Every recommendation on this site is a place one of us has actually visited, usually more than once.

  • 02

    We write for the return trip

    We favor the details you'd want on your second visit — timing, transit, and the quiet hour — over generic highlight lists.

  • 03

    We keep the list short

    One strong beach, one strong temple, one strong country pick — curated, not exhaustive.

Our mission

To help travelers spend less time filtering search results and more time packing — by handing over a short, honest list of places worth the plane ticket.
